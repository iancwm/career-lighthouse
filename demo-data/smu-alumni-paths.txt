SMU Career Paths: Alumni Success Stories

INVESTMENT BANKING
SMU Lee Kong Chian School of Business (Finance) → Goldman Sachs Singapore (IBD Analyst)
Typical path: Strong GPA (3.8+), CFA Level 1 before graduation, SMU Investment Club leadership.
SMU typically places 5-8 students annually across all BB IBD desks in Singapore. Key firms: GS, MS, JP Morgan, Citi, UBS.
Timeline: Apply in Year 2/3 summer for internships. Full-time offers come from internship conversion (80% rate at GS Singapore 2024).

PUBLIC SECTOR / STATUTORY BOARDS
SMU any faculty → GIC / Temasek / MAS
GIC recruits from SMU across all faculties. Technology roles from SCIS, investment roles from Business/Economics.
PSC Scholarship holders from SMU have strong placement into MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore).
GIC 2024: SMU among top 3 feeder schools for Singapore graduate hires. Interview process: 3 rounds, case study + fit.

CONSULTING
SMU → McKinsey / BCG / Bain Singapore
MBB in Singapore recruit ~10-12 SMU students per year combined. Target: Business, Economics, SCIS.
McKinsey Singapore values SMU's seminar-style communication training — SMU candidates consistently score highly on structured communication.
Bain Singapore has a dedicated SMU campus recruiting relationship. Apply by September for July start.

TECHNOLOGY
SMU SCIS → Google / Meta / Bytedance Singapore
Google Singapore: 10+ SMU SCIS hires annually. Focus on SWE and data roles.
Leetcode proficiency (Medium) required. SMU ICPC team members have strong conversion.
Bytedance Singapore growing fast; strong appetite for SMU information systems graduates.
